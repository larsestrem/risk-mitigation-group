Risk Mitigation Group, LLC

Risk Mitigation Group is the parent company for the websites below. We collect, analyze and share risk information to help build safer communities.

Risk-Mitigation.Group - Parent company of websites listed below.

Insurance.Report - A service designed to help insurance agents find the nearest fire station and nearest fire hydrant for insurance inspections and underwriting. When either a hydrant or fire station can't be found, insurance professions often call the fire station requesting more information. Insurance.Report services saves a significant amount of time for the insurance industry and firefighters.

FireDepartment.net - Helping fire departments get online, recruit volunteers & share information with the communities they serve. Fire administrators and firefighters can scroll to the bottom of their fire department profile and request to manage their fire department's profile. Community members and insurance agents can also suggest additions and updates that will be reviewed before being added to the database.

FireEngineAcademy.com - Resource for new fire engine operators to learn about operating a fire engine. Topics include pump discharge pressure and fire engine pump and plumbing. We are looking for a fire academy interested in publishing content to FireEngineAcademy.com and FrictionLossCalculator.com in exchange for marketing to a combined 17,000 visitors a month.

FrictionLossCalculator.com - Resource for new fire engine operators to learn about fluid dynamics. Friction loss is one of the more difficult topics for new fire engine operators. We simplified the process and provided multiple ways to solve friction loss calculations. We are looking for a fire academy interested in publishing content to FireEngineAcademy.com and FrictionLossCalculator.com in exchange for marketing to over 15,000 visitors a month.

NVfirechf.org - We provide fire department information to the official Nevada Fire Chiefs Association website. Fire department contact information is subject to firedepartment.net's terms of service and privacy policy.
